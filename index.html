His early practice was a broad based litigation practice. This included commercial, employment, real estate, estate, and family litigation. A resident of Penticton since 2007, his practice has, for the past several years, been focused on solicitors work. This includes company law, commercial law, wills, estates and real estate. He believes that thoughtful and careful planning is essential to avoid misunderstandings and disputes in the future. He is committed to providing his clients with practical long term solutions.
